About Us

At Studio Zones, we are a dynamic new force in architectural animation, web development, and graphic design. As a fresh and innovative company, we are committed to elevating your brand with cutting-edge 360-degree campaigns. Our recent project, a comprehensive campaign for The Court Skyscraper, a premier metropolitan landmark, showcases our dedication to excellence. Our team meticulously captured every detail, from light and texture to finish, delivering a stunning and realistic result. By applying a holistic marketing approach, we’ve positioned the brand effectively. Our services include Brand Identity, Realistic 3D Views, 2D and 3D Floor Plans, Print and Outdoor Design, and Realistic 3D Architectural Walkthroughs. We are here to ensure your vision is beautifully represented across all media, both social and electronic.
